You are cordially invited to join us in Ostende on Friday 27th September for the launch of our new Boeing 747-400 Freighter aircraft

On behalf of ANA Airline Management and the whole Network Aviation Group, we are pleased to invite you to the launch party of our new Boeing 747-400 Freighter aircraft that joins our fleet on Friday 27th September 2013.


We will be celebrating with drinks and a buffet lunch as well as a closer inspection of the aircraft with a guided tour to show of its capabilities.

The aircraft will be based in Ostende, Belgium alongside our existing fleet of
MD11F aircraft and will operate on some of our partner airlines scheduled routes
as well as being available for ad-hoc charters on a worldwide basis.
The aircraft is a factory built B747-400F with both nose and side doors
and with a maximum capacity of 110,000 Kilos Net.


Please see below for further details regarding this exciting day out.

We very much look forward to you joining us on this historic day.
